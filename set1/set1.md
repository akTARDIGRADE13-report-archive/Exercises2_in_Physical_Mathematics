## Problem 1

### (a)

$$
\begin{aligned}
e^{-2x} &= 1 - 2x + \frac{4x^2}{2!} - \frac{8x^3}{3!} + \frac{16x^4}{4!} - \frac{32x^5}{5!} + \cdots \\
&= \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} (-2)^n \frac{x^n}{n!} .
\end{aligned}
$$

### (b)

$$
(1+x)^3 = 1 + 3x + 3x^2 + x^3 = \sum_{n=0}^{3} \binom{3}{n} x^n .
$$

### (c)

If $0 < r < 1$ ,

$$
\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} r^n = \frac{1}{1-r} .
$$

Therefore,

$$
\frac{1}{1-x} = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} x^n .
$$
